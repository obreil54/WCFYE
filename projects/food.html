Food

Helping feed food-deprived families and children.

Without proper resources, nutritious meals and a steady supply of food, no community can prosper. In Yemen, hunger and starvation surge at unprecended levels that can lead to famine. More than 20 million people in Yemen are currently food insecure, including women and children, and are in dire need of humanitarian assistance.

Almost 2 million children are facing malnutrition and are prone to sickness and weakness. So far, thousands of lives have been lost to starvation. With food prices and inflation on the rise, the stability of their well-being depends on outside aid.

Years of war and poverty have torn down Yemen's livelihood and economy, resulting in acute food insecurity and malnutrion. Hence, we must come together and help feed the hungry to help and secure a bright future in Yemen.

Their lives can see betterment, and our collective efforts can make that possible. They are waiting for our aid to make a difference.

What Needs To Be Done?

WCFYE's Food Project delivers food parcels and daily meals to feed hungry families across the country. As we continue our mission of feeding those in need, your generosity and contributions can come a long way, making your continuous support crucial in these times of crisis.

For Only £60, You Can Feed A Starving Family For A Whole Month!

Food parcels cover the basic requirements of needy families. Each parcel consists of flour, rice, sugar, beans and oil.

For Only £3, You Can Provide A Daily Food Meal And Feed A Starving Family!

Daily food meals contain bread, vegetables, fruits and yoghurt.
