Livelihood

Empowering Orhan Families and Renewing the Hope.

When the chances of orphan families to survive are limited due to war, they become dependent on others.

In Yemen, the war has put an extra burden on orphan families. By supporting them to restore their confidence and hope through economic opportunities, they will improve their quality of life.

WCFYE is working on the ground to help orphan families to stand and depend on themselves by providing them sewing machines with its necessities to start work and be able to provide for themselves.
